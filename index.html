Multi-instrumentalist Logan Dyer creates a unique soundscape for any atmosphere by combining elements of many musical genres. Using live phrase looping, he often sounds like 2 or 3 musicians at once. The early show is acoustic guitar based and a good fit for restaurants, mid sized bars and family friendly events. The late show is an electric guitar experience intended to be the main event and will keep your party going. Logan Dyer's Jazz performance showcases the saxophone and other wind instruments with a mix of standards and originals.
